可食用植物疫苗接种体验品牌设计

在新型病毒出现频率高、疫苗接种日益成为社会焦点的现状下，我们关注到儿童疫苗接种这一主题，发现儿童疫苗接种体验有待改善以及向儿童科普疫苗相关知识的必要性。因此，我们尝试为儿童这一人群提供更好的疫苗接种体验。

一方面，我们利用基于植物的mRNA技术和分子农业中植物瞬时转染系统技术进行植物疫苗的生产，将蔬菜与疫苗结合，提供儿童更易接受的可食用疫苗；另一方面，我们设计了新型的接种体验流程，并在流程中融入疫苗接种相关知识以及防疫知识的科普。
 
同时，我们发现可食用疫苗在生产运输上具有极大优势。因此，我们还将利用可食用疫苗进行能够满足未来灾难情况下人类需求的疫苗应急产品的提供。 

In the current situation where the frequency of new viruses is high and vaccination is increasingly becoming the focus of society, we have paid attention to the theme of children's vaccination and found that children's vaccination experience needs to be improved and it is necessary to popularize vaccination-related knowledge among children. Therefore, we try to provide a better vaccination experience for children as a group.
On the one hand, we use plant-based mRNA technology and plant transient transfection system technology in molecular agriculture to produce plant vaccines, combining vegetables and vaccines to provide edible vaccines that are more acceptable to children; on the other hand, we have designed a new type of vaccine. Experience the process of vaccination, and integrate knowledge of vaccination and epidemic prevention into the process.
At the same time, we found that edible vaccines have great advantages in production and transportation. Therefore, we will also use edible vaccines to provide vaccine emergency products that can meet human needs in future disaster situations.
